About Me

   Aly Sweet (yes that is her real name) began doing acroyoga in 2016 by accident. After the initial shock, Aly fell in love. She found personal growth and joy in building balance, strength, and partnership.


After years of playing, training, and helping guide new practitioners at jams, Aly became an AcroLove certified teacher in 2020. She is passionate about sharing her love of acroyoga and growing her community.
